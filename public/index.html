Listening Test : Violin Comparison

Thank you for taking part in this listening test!

The task is straightforward. In each round, you will first listen to two reference recordings, labeled A and B, played on two different violins. You will then hear a third recording, X.

Although X is a different recording, it was played on the same violin as either A or B. Your task is to decide which one.

Please select “X is A” or “X is B”, then move on to the next round by clicking the arrow in the bottom-right corner.

Before starting the test, please provide the following information:

Are you a musician?
Are you a luthier?

Click Save, confirm, then name your JSON file.
